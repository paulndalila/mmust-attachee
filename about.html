About Us

Masinde Muliro University of Science & Technology (MMUST) is committed to providing top-notch education and practical experience to students both within and outside the university. Our industrial attachment program is designed to equip students with hands-on experience, enhancing their skills and preparing them for the competitive job market.

Why Choose MMUST for Industrial Attachment?

Experienced Faculty

Learn from experienced faculty members who are experts in their fields and dedicated to student success.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Gain practical experience using our state-of-the-art facilities and equipment, ensuring you are industry-ready.

Diverse Departments
